Privacy & Safety

This tool is designed to be simple and privacy-friendly:

  • Everything runs in your browser. By default, nothing is stored when you leave or refresh the page.
  • If you turn on "Save my data on this device", your card names, limits, balances, and tool settings (target %, allocation strategy, remainder behavior, and available payment amount) are saved locally in this browser using localStorage.
  • No data is uploaded, tracked, or shared with any external server, and there is no login or account system.
